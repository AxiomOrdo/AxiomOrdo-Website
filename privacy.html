Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how AxiomOrdo collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data. It applies to information you provide through this website or by contacting AxiomOrdo directly.

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Contact email: pfas@axiomordo.com

Who we are

AxiomOrdo provides PFAS-related compliance and defensibility services. For the purposes of data protection law, AxiomOrdo is the controller of the personal data described in this notice.

What personal data we collect

We may collect and use personal data that you provide when you contact us, including:

  • your name;
  • your business email address;
  • your company name;
  • your job title or role;
  • details of your enquiry;
  • information about your products, suppliers, or compliance concerns, where that information identifies you or another individual.

We may also receive limited technical information from the operation of the website and hosting environment, such as standard server request data needed to deliver and secure the site.

How we collect personal data

We currently collect personal data when:

  • you send an email to AxiomOrdo using the contact details or enquiry links on this website;
  • you contact us directly about our services;
  • technical request data is generated through normal access to the website.

Why we use your personal data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • to respond to your enquiry;
  • to assess whether our services are relevant to your organisation;
  • to communicate with you about potential work, proposals, or next steps;
  • to maintain business records;
  • to operate, secure, and improve the website.

Lawful basis for processing

We process personal data where this is necessary for our legitimate interests, including operating the business, responding to enquiries, assessing potential client needs, and maintaining appropriate records.

Where processing is necessary in order to take steps at your request before entering into a contract, we may also rely on that basis.

Who we share personal data with

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share personal data with service providers where necessary to operate the business and website, including:

  • email service providers;
  • website hosting or infrastructure providers;
  • professional advisers, where necessary for legal, regulatory, or business administration purposes.

We only share personal data where there is a valid reason to do so.

International transfers

Some service providers may process personal data outside the UK. Where this happens, we expect appropriate safeguards to be in place in line with applicable data protection requirements.

How long we keep personal data

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including responding to enquiries, managing potential business relationships, and maintaining appropriate business records.

Enquiry data that does not lead to ongoing business may typically be retained for up to 12 months, unless a longer period is justified for legal, regulatory, or record-keeping reasons.

If you become a client, relevant data may be retained for longer in line with contractual, legal, regulatory, and accounting requirements.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have the right to:

  • request access to your personal data;
  • request correction of inaccurate personal data;
  • request deletion of your personal data;
  • object to certain processing;
  • request restriction of processing;
  • request transfer of your personal data, where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: pfas@axiomordo.com

Complaints

If you have concerns about how your personal data is handled, please contact AxiomOrdo first. You may also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office in the UK.

Cookies and analytics

This website is not currently intended to use analytics, advertising trackers, or newsletter signup tools. If this changes, this notice will be updated.

Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.

This notice should be reviewed and updated before adding contact forms, analytics, cookies, CRM tools, newsletter signup, or any other data collection features.
